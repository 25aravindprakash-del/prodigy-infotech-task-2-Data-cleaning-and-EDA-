Registry Log & Historical Context

The Titanic passenger registry holds the verified ledger entries for the travelers on board the White Star Line steamer during its maiden voyage. In reconstructing this historical record, we have performed the following steps to reconcile incomplete logs:

  • Record Reconciliation: Resolved incomplete fields (such as missing ages, boarding ports, and cabin numbers) to restore ledger integrity.
  • Demographic Profiling: Modeled age brackets and boarding classes to identify patterns of survival.
  • Reconstructed Ledgers: Formulated relationships between passenger details and survival status to understand the impact of cabin allocations and deck positioning.
